Retree

Retree is an editor for tree reconstruction and visualization. It can automatically or manually trim redundant branches and leaves, customize leaf models and trunk textures, and build meshes with a flexible constructor. By taking a point cloud as input and leveraging the powerful AdTree reconstruction algorithm, generates accurate tree skeletons for further editing and rendering.
